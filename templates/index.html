What is this?

This app reads monophonic music score sheets, interprets the notes and translates them into the following formats:

  • Solfege (Do, Re, Mi)
  • Letter (C, D, E)
  • Number / Cipher (1, 2, 3)

*Monophonic: Music Having a single melodic line

Who is this for?

This app is recommended for self-teaching musicians who have a minimum knowledge base of at least one musical notation format mentioned above.
